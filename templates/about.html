About This Website

This website was created by Tobin Schleifer with the goal of helping people track their workouts and set personal fitness goals.

Whether you're just starting out or have been training for a while, this platform allows you to:

  • Log your daily workouts
  • View an exercise library for ideas
  • Track your progress
  • Set and review fitness goals

The website is designed to be simple, efficient, and accessible for anyone who wants to improve their fitness journey.
